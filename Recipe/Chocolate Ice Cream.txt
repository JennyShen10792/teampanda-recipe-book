Description: 
This chocolate ice cream is a rich, custard-style ice cream with no raw eggs!

Ingredients: 
1 cup mild, 1 cup sugar, 3 egg yolk, 2 ounces chocolate

Step-by-step cooking instructions: 
1. 1 Combine milk, sugar, cocoa powder, and salt in a saucepan over medium heat. Bring to a simmer, stirring constantly.
2. 2 Place egg yolks into a small bowl. Gradually whisk in about 1/2 cup of hot milk mixture, then return to the saucepan. Heat and stir until thickened, but do not boil.
3. 3 Remove from the heat and stir in chopped chocolate until melted.
4. 
5. 4 Pour into a chilled bowl and refrigerate, stirring occasionally, until cold, about 2 hours.
